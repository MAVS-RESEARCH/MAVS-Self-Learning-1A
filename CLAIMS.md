# Claim Boundary

The authoritative generated claim ledger is `results/perception_closure_v04/phase10/claims/claim_ledger.json`.

Supported within the audited finite scope:

- executable differentiated diagnostic synthesis for the Phase 6 grammar, operations, banks, and lifecycle;
- Perception Closure for declared covered classes and resource budgets;
- cumulative three-generation transfer on the sealed Phase 9 blind bank.

Unsupported:

- universal zero error or zero escalation outside the certified covered class;
- production deployment readiness;
- general-domain or frontier-model superiority.

Oracle conditions are evaluator-only bounds and are never competitive claims. Earlier Phase 3 evidence validates lifecycle plumbing, not genuine differentiated synthesis. Rejected Phase 10 runs are diagnostic-only and cannot elevate a claim.
